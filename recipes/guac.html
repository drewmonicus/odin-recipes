Guacamole

Homemade guacamole you can make in under 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 Avocados
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Half of a onion
  • Lime Juice
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Scoop out avocado into a bowl and mash until texture is mushy but still with chunks
  2. Finely chop half of a onion and dice up tomatoes
  3. Squeeze juice out of one small lime
  4. Pick out and cut cilantro
  5. Add and mix all ingredients together and add salt and pepper to taste
