Ramen

Simple instant ramen dish.

This recipe is a simple instant ramen dish that you could make in under 5 minutes. All you will need is instant ramen and for extra toppings, you can add a slice of processed american cheese to make the soup base thicker or one whole egg to make the ramen have a creamier taste. It is recommended to add them right after pouring the boiling water and let sit for at least 2 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen Packet
  • Scallions
  • Cheese (Optional)
  • 1 Egg (Optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 2-3 cups of water
  2. Chop scallions finely
  3. Add the boiling water to the noodles
  4. Put in the seasoning and vegetable packet
  5. Add egg or cheese and let sit for 2 minutes
